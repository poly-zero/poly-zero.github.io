If you've seen a picture of the great Pacific Garbage Patch then you already know that plastic waste is a huge problem for the planet.

And the problem is not limited to plastic waste; the creation, transportation, and even the process of recycling plastic all emit huge amounts of CO2 into the atmosphere.

See: The Hidden Costs of a Plastic Planet

As consumers the most important steps we can take involve reducing the amount of single-use plastics we consume. Steps such as:

  • Always taking your own bags to the grocery store.
  • Using reusable bottles or tumblers for drinks.
  • Eliminating the use of plastic straws.
That said, depending on where you live, it may be extremely difficult to eliminate plastic completely. In that case, one way we can reduce our environmental impact is by of-setting our emissions.

PolyZero was created to help consumers calculate the CO2 footprint emitted from single-use plastics and off-set the emissions.
