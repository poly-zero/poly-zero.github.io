"The plastic pollution crisis overwhelming our oceans is also a significant and growing threat to the Earth's climate...

Nearly every piece of plastic begins as a fossil fuel, and greenhouse gases are emitted at each stage of the plastic lifecycle:

  1. fossil fuel extraction and transport,
  2. plastic refining and manufacture,
  3. managing plastic waste, and
  4. its ongoing impact in our oceans, waterways, and landscape...

At current levels, greenhouse gas emissions from the plastic lifecycle threaten the ability of the global community to keep global temperature rise below 1.5°C degrees. By 2050, the greenhouse gas emissions from plastic could reach over 56 gigatons—10-13 percent of the entire remaining carbon budget."

Source:
Center for International Environmental Law (CIEL)
Plastic & Climate: The Hidden Costs of a Plastic Planet

Depending on where you live, it may be difficult to reduce your plastic footprint, let alone eliminate plastic completely.

Beyond reducing the plastic we consume, one way we can reduce our environmental impact is by off-setting our emissions.

PolyZero was created to help Japan-based consumers estimate the CO2 footprint emitted from the single-use plastics they consume in their daily life and then off-set the emissions.

The goal of our app is first to raise awareness and provide advice and tools for reducing your plastic footprint and second, to provide a simple mechanism to purchase CO2 off-sets to reduce the climate impact of the plastics that we are unable to eliminate from our lives.

If you'd like to find out more, or even contribute to our open source applicatrion code, please visit our GitHub: https://github.com/poly-zero
